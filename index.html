Profile/ Bio

Buildote Directors...

Timilehin Daniel Fatoki is a civil engineer and construction project management professional who serves as Director of Buildote Solutions Limited. He holds an HND in Civil Engineering from The Polytechnic, Ibadan and completed construction project management training at Columbia University (New York). Combining practical site experience with disciplined project controls, Timilehin drives Buildote’s commitment to safety, quality and long-term value. He specialises in structural works, road construction and drainage systems, and oversees technical design review, value engineering, HSE compliance and stakeholder liaison to ensure projects meet specifications, budgets and timelines. Timilehin leads a collaborative delivery model, working with certified engineers and industry professionals where required to guarantee regulatory compliance and high workmanship standards.

At Buildote, Timilehin believes good construction is more than building — it’s responsibility, clarity and accountability. Prospective clients are invited to request a capability statement, free site assessment or project consultation to discuss how Buildote can deliver durable, cost-effective infrastructure solutions.



Fatoki Folashade Stakeholder | Head of Building Materials Supply Fatoki Folashade is a key stakeholder at Buildote Solutions Limited, responsible for overseeing building materials supply and procurement. She brings strong market expertise and practical leadership to ensure every project is supported with reliable, standard-compliant materials. A respected entrepreneur and recognised market leader, Folashade currently serves as a Market Women Secretary, reflecting her credibility, organisational capacity, and deep-rooted trust within the construction materials market.

At Buildote, she manages supplier coordination, material verification, pricing control, and delivery logistics. Her role ensures that construction sites receive the right materials at the right time—reducing delays, controlling costs, and maintaining quality standards. By connecting trusted markets with site operations, Folashade strengthens Buildote’s ability to deliver projects that are durable, transparent in cost, and professionally executed.




Omoye Mary Thomas Tech Lead | Civil Engineer | Digital Communications Manager. Omoye Mary Thomas is the Tech Lead and Digital Communications Manager at Buildote Solutions Limited. A trained civil engineer with a strong interest in technology, she bridges engineering expertise and digital systems to support the company’s operations and client engagement. She oversees Buildote’s website, digital infrastructure, and online platforms, ensuring that information is accurate, accessible, and aligned with professional standards. Through clear digital communication, Omoye helps present Buildote’s engineering services in a way that is easy for clients to understand and trust.

In her role, she manages digital content strategy and audience engagement, translating technical construction processes into clear, client-focused messaging. Her work supports transparency, improves client understanding of project workflows, and strengthens Buildote’s professional image across digital channels. Omoye’s combination of civil engineering knowledge, technical coordination, and digital communication plays a key role in reinforcing Buildote’s credibility and connecting the company to clients in a modern, reliable manner.

About Us

We Don’t Just Build Structures. We Engineer Landmarks.

Buildote Solutions Limited – Buildote Solutions Limited is a CAC-registered construction, civil engineering, and real estate development company delivering high-quality building and infrastructure solutions across Nigeria and beyond. We serve both public and private sector clients with a strong commitment to structural integrity, cost efficiency, safety, and timely delivery. Our core services cover building construction, civil engineering works, road construction, structural works, renovation, remodeling, and general infrastructural development. We also operate as real estate developers, property managers, and estate agents, providing end-to-end solutions from concept and feasibility to construction and property delivery. Buildote Solutions Limited offers project management, site supervision, architectural and structural support, interior and exterior finishing, procurement, and supply of construction materials. We are actively engaged in the importation, distribution, and supply of cement, sand, aggregates, steel, tiles, roofing materials, plumbing and electrical fittings, and other construction inputs, ensuring quality control and cost certainty for our clients.

We undertake turnkey construction projects, government and private sector contracts, joint ventures, and strategic partnerships. Our expertise also extends to engineering consultancy, quantity surveying, cost estimation, feasibility studies, environmental impact assessment (EIA), site safety management, and sustainable construction practices. At Buildote Solutions Limited, we combine technical excellence with practical execution. Our approach is simple: clear communication, professional supervision, durable construction, and client satisfaction. We build structures that last — and relationships that grow.
